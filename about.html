Who We Are

Lucent Line Transport L.L.C

Lucent Line Transport L.L.C is a UAE-based logistics company specializing in large-scale trucking solutions across the Middle East.

We deliver international shipments with efficiency, reliability, and professionalism — helping global trading companies move goods seamlessly across borders.

Our diverse service portfolio includes heavy-lift shipping, air freight, sea freight, warehousing, and customs clearance. With our commitment to quality, we ensure smooth operations by preventing regional bottlenecks and delivering on time, every time.
