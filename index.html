Who We Are

Lucent Line Transport L.L.C

Lucent Line Transport L.L.C is a UAE-based logistics company specializing in large-scale trucking solutions across the Middle East. We deliver international shipments with efficiency, reliability, and professionalism, helping global trading companies move goods seamlessly across borders.

With a diverse service portfolio including heavy-lift shipping, air freight, sea freight, warehousing, and customs clearance, we ensure smooth operations by preventing regional bottlenecks.
